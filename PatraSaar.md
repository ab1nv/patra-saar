## What is PatraSaar?

PatraSaar is an AI-powered legal intelligence platform built exclusively for the Indian legal system. It allows advocates, law firms, businesses, and citizens to upload any Indian legal document — a contract, FIR, court order, legal notice, or statute — and instantly receive plain-language explanations, risk assessments, and verified citations, all grounded in Indian law.

PatraSaar is not a chatbot. It is a structured legal intelligence suite that understands the language of Indian courts, cross-references the Indian Penal Code, BNS, CrPC, and thousands of Supreme Court and High Court precedents — and presents answers the way a Senior Advocate would explain them to a client.

---

## The Problem PatraSaar Solves

Indian legal documents are notoriously complex. A single FIR can reference multiple IPC sections. A commercial contract may contain clauses that violate the Indian Contract Act without either party realising it. A legal notice may have a 30-day response window buried in paragraph seven.

Today, most people have three options:

1. **Hire a lawyer** — expensive, slow, and inaccessible for most Indians
2. **Search online** — generic results, no document-specific analysis
3. **Use ChatGPT or a generic AI** — dangerous because it has no grounding in Indian law, fabricates case citations, and gives the same generic answer to every document

PatraSaar is none of these. It is purpose-built for India.

---

## Why PatraSaar is Fundamentally Different from Using ChatGPT

This is the most important distinction to understand.

### 1. ChatGPT Does Not Know Your Document. PatraSaar Does.

When you paste a contract into ChatGPT, it reads it once, has no memory of it, and cannot tell you exactly _where_ in the document a risk exists. PatraSaar indexes your document and can point to the exact paragraph, clause, and page number that is problematic.

### 2. ChatGPT Hallucinates Case Law. PatraSaar Cites Verified Sources.

This is critical. ChatGPT is known to invent case names, fake Supreme Court judgements, and fabricate IPC section numbers with complete confidence. PatraSaar uses a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) architecture — meaning every answer it gives is grounded in a verified knowledge base of Indian statutes and case law. Every citation links back to a real source. Every claim has a confidence score.

### 3. ChatGPT Understands American Law by Default. PatraSaar Understands Indian Law Natively.

ChatGPT is trained predominantly on Western legal content. It does not understand the nuance between IPC and BNS, does not know the difference between cognizable and non-cognizable offences under CrPC, and cannot map old IPC sections to their new BNS equivalents. PatraSaar was built from the ground up around Indian statutes, Indian courts, and Indian legal workflows.

### 4. ChatGPT Has No Legal Workflow. PatraSaar Is a Complete Legal Workspace.

ChatGPT gives you a chat window. PatraSaar gives you:

- A **Document Summary Card** the moment you upload — parties, key dates, obligations, risk flags
- **Clause-level annotations** with plain-language explanations
- **Risk scoring** on contract clauses
- **BNS/IPC cross-referencing** — automatically maps old IPC sections to new BNS provisions
- **Case folders** to group related documents (FIR + chargesheet + bail order)
- **Sovereign Citations** — a verified source panel showing exactly which case law or statute backs every answer
- **Context Confidence Score** — tells you how certain the system is about its answer

### 5. ChatGPT Forgets Everything. PatraSaar Remembers Your Cases.

Every research session in PatraSaar is saved. You can return to a case folder days later, continue your analysis, compare documents, and track what questions have been asked. ChatGPT starts from zero every time.

## What PatraSaar Is Not

PatraSaar is not a substitute for legal advice. It is a legal intelligence tool. It explains, analyses, and researches — it does not represent you in court or file documents on your behalf. Every response includes clear sourcing so you and your lawyer can verify every claim independently.
